About the Department

The Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) Department of is one of the leading technology-focused departments in Bangladesh. It is dedicated to providing students with a strong foundation in computer science, programming, software development, artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and other emerging technologies. The department combines theoretical knowledge with practical experience through modern laboratories, industry-oriented projects, workshops, and research activities. Its curriculum is regularly updated to meet the demands of the rapidly evolving IT industry.

The CSE Department also emphasizes innovation, creativity, and professional development. Students are encouraged to participate in programming contests, hackathons, research projects, seminars, and technology clubs to enhance their technical and problem-solving skills. The department maintains strong connections with the software industry, offering internship opportunities and career guidance to prepare graduates for successful careers. With experienced faculty members, modern facilities, and a supportive learning environment, the CSE Department of Daffodil International University helps students become skilled professionals and responsible contributors to the global technology community.

Offered Courses

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) offers a wide range of courses designed to build students' knowledge and technical skills in the field of computing. The curriculum includes fundamental subjects such as Programming, Data Structures, Algorithms, Computer Networks, Database Management Systems, Operating Systems, Software Engineering, Web Development, Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Computer Architecture. In addition to technical courses, students also study Mathematics, Physics, English, Communication Skills, and other general education subjects to develop analytical thinking and professional competence. The courses are carefully designed to combine theoretical concepts with hands-on laboratory work, projects, and practical applications, ensuring that graduates are well-prepared for higher studies and successful careers in the software and technology industries.
